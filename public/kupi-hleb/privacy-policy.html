Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the купи хлеб (Buybread) app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Nuril Baradusov (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application uses your Apple iCloud account to store and share drawings with your paired friend. No other personal information is collected. Registration is not required to use the Application — it uses your existing iCloud account.

iCloud Data

The Application uses Apple's iCloud service to enable sharing drawings between paired users. The following data is stored in iCloud:

  • Drawings you create and send
  • Pairing information to connect with your friend

This data is stored on Apple's iCloud servers and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Data is only shared with the specific user you pair with — it is not publicly accessible or shared with any other third parties.

The Service Provider does not have access to your iCloud data.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Your drawings are only shared with the friend you explicitly pair with using the Application. No data is shared with any other third parties. The Service Provider does not have access to your drawings or iCloud data.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

To delete your data from iCloud, you can unpair within the Application or manage your iCloud storage through your device settings.

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. Your drawings and pairing data are protected by Apple's iCloud security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest.
